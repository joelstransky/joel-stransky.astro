While working at the Learning Innovation Branch of the Military Intelligence Brigade we were asked to develop a new badge for MI certified instructors.
This was a real growth piece for me since military imagery is steeped in symbolism. Great care was required to research symbols that were not only devoid of controversy, but underscored the values of both civilian and enlisted instructors. Every element of this design from the colors to the number of elements celebrate the bravery and dedication of such an esteemed organization.
