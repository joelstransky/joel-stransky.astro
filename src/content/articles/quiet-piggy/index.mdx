### The Project

I honestly just wanted to see what Gemini 3 could do. Could it act as a partner in a complete creative process?

The spark came from a real place. A public official made a statement that hurt a lot of people, and I felt a need to respond. But I also know how side projects go—if it feels like work, it stalls. It had to be effortless, or it wasn't getting done.

This book is the result. A quick, heartfelt response brought to life by new technology.

### AI & The Industry

I'm acutely aware of the disruption AI is causing in the creative industry—it affects me too.

I want to be clear: this wasn't a gig someone lost. This was a personal project with a binary outcome—either I finished it in a weekend, or it wouldn't exist at all. I felt the message was important enough to get out there, and my hope is that the optics of this response might still achieve its desired effect.
